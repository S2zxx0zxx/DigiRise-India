DigiRise India — Do Not Track Policy

DigiRise India respects browser Do Not Track (DNT) signals.

When a visitor's browser sends a DNT:1 signal, DigiRise India will not:
- Load third-party advertising trackers
- Share visitor behavior data with third-party ad networks

DigiRise India may still use essential, first-party analytics required for basic site functionality (e.g. counting page visits) regardless of DNT signal, as these do not involve cross-site tracking.
