Kab Khud Karna Sahi Hai? — Honest Answer

✅ Khud Karo Jab...
  • Monthly revenue ₹1 lakh se kam ho
  • Business abhi 0-6 months purana ho
  • Sirf 1-2 social platforms chahiye
  • Budget ₹5,000/month se kam ho
  • Tum already marketing mein interested ho
  • Product-market fit abhi dhundh rahe ho
  • Part-time business ho — side hustle
🚀 Agency Hire Karo Jab...
  • Monthly revenue ₹2 lakh+ ho
  • Marketing pe time nahi hai
  • Competitors aage nikal rahe hain
  • Ads already try kiye par results nahi
  • Multiple platforms simultaneously chahiye
  • Fast results chahiye — 60 days mein
  • Website + Ads + SEO sab ek saath

✅ DigiRise India ka Honest Advice: Agar abhi revenue ₹50,000/month se kam hai — pehle product/service perfect karo, basic social media khud karo, GMB setup karo (free). Jab ₹1-2 lakh/month ho jaye, tab agency se baat karo. Yeh sahi sequence hai.

Agency Choose Karte Waqt 6 Red Flags

  • 🚩
    "Page 1 Guaranteed in 30 days" — SEO mein koi guarantee nahi hoti. Jo yeh bolte hain woh ya toh jhooth bolte hain ya black-hat techniques use karte hain jo baad mein penalize hoti hain.
  • 🚩
    No Case Studies with Real Numbers — "We grew XYZ brand" likhna kaafi nahi. Exact metrics maango — kitne followers, kitna ROI, kitna revenue increase.
  • 🚩
    Vague Pricing — "Custom Quote Only" — Transparent agency hamesha starting price batati hai. Hidden costs ka sign hai vague pricing.
  • 🚩
    No Contract / Agreement — Koi bhi professional agency written agreement deti hai — scope of work, deliverables, timeline, payment terms.
  • 🚩
    Slow Response Time — Agar agency hire karne se pehle 2-3 din reply karne mein lagate hain, toh hire karne ke baad aur slow honge.
  • 🚩
    Fake Followers / Fake Reviews — Agency ke apne social media accounts check karo. Agar unke followers engagement nahi kar rahe — red flag hai.
