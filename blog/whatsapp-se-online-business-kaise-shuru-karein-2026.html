Setup Steps — 30 Minutes Ka Kaam

1
Download aur Register

Play Store/App Store se "WhatsApp Business" download karo. Business phone number se register karo. Existing WhatsApp number migrate kar sakte ho — contacts preserve honge.

💡 Separate business number best hai — personal aur business mix mat karo. Airtel/Jio dual SIM use karo.
2
Business Profile Fill Karo — Completely

Settings → Business Tools → Business Profile:
• Business Name: Exact business name (change nahi hoga baad mein easily)
• Category: Relevant category choose karo
• Description: 139 chars — kya karte ho, kaise help karte ho, contact karo kab
• Address, Website, Email, Hours — sab fill karo
• Profile Photo: Business logo ya professional photo

💡 Incomplete profile = less trust = fewer conversions. 100% complete profile = professional first impression.
3
Labels Setup Karo — Contacts Organize Karo

Settings → Business Tools → Labels. Yeh labels create karo shuruat mein:
🔵 New Customer — pehli baar contact kiya
🟡 Interested — poochha lekin buy nahi kiya
🟠 Pending Payment — order confirm, payment pending
🟢 Order Complete — purchase done
⭐ VIP Customer — repeat buyer, high value

4
Link Generate Karo — Share Everywhere

Settings → Business Tools → Short Link. Tumhara unique WhatsApp link milega — wa.me/91XXXXXXXXXX. Isko Instagram bio mein, Facebook page mein, Google My Business mein, visiting card mein — har jagah daalo.

💡 Custom message add karo link mein: wa.me/91XXXXXXXXXX?text=Hi%2C+I+want+to+order — customer automatically message leke aayega.

Broadcast Strategy — 1000 Contacts Tak Pahuncho

⚡ Quick Answer

Broadcast List = ek message → 256 contacts ko simultaneously → individual delivery (group nahi — private feel). Sab contacts tumhara number save kiye hue honge toh message milega. Multiple lists banao — customers, leads, VIPs. Best time: Evening 7-9 PM.

Broadcast Lists — Kaise Organize Karein

🟢
Active Customers List
Highest Priority

Jinse pehle purchase hua. Weekly updates, exclusive offers, loyalty discounts. Sabse high conversion rate.

🔵
Warm Leads List
Follow-up Focus

Jinhoone inquiry kiya lekin buy nahi kiya. Value-first messages bhejo — tips, guides, testimonials. Nurture karo.

⭐
VIP Customers
Special Treatment

Repeat buyers, high-value orders. First access to new products, special discounts, personalized service.

🔴
Lapsed Customers
Win-Back Campaign

3+ mahine se kuch nahi kharida. "We miss you" offer bhejo — discount ya free shipping. 20-30% win-back possible.

Broadcast Best Practices

  • Frequency: Maximum 2-3 baar per week — zyada = unsubscribe/block
  • Best time: Tuesday-Thursday, 7-9 PM IST — highest open rate
  • Message length: Short + clear + one CTA. 5 lines maximum
  • Personalize: "Hi [Name]" — WhatsApp pe naam possible nahi lekin segment karo minimum
  • Value-first: Har message mein kuch useful — tip, offer, news
  • Emoji use karo: Emojis se open rate 15-25% improve hoti hai

8 Common Mistakes — Jo WhatsApp Business Mein Hote Hain

❌ Mistake 1: Personal Account Se Business Chalana
✅ Sahi: WhatsApp Business App — separate, professional, extra features

💡 Personal account = no catalog, no auto-reply, no analytics. Business App = free upgrade instant.

❌ Mistake 2: Spam Broadcasting — Roz 5 Messages
✅ Sahi: Maximum 2-3 baar per week — value-first content

💡 Spamming = block = number ban. Quality over quantity — har message valuable hona chahiye.

❌ Mistake 3: Low Quality Photos — Catalog Mein
✅ Sahi: Bright, clear, square photos — natural light mein lo

💡 Dark/blurry photos = professional nahi lagta = conversions kam. 5 minute extra photo ke liye = 50% zyada orders.

❌ Mistake 4: Price Nahi Batana — "DM for price"
✅ Sahi: Catalog mein exact price daalo — transparency = trust

💡 Price chhupana = buyers ko irritate karta hai. Transparent pricing = more inquiries, less time waste.

❌ Mistake 5: Reply Mein Ghante Lagana
✅ Sahi: Auto-reply setup karo + maximum 30-minute response

💡 India mein buyer ka attention 10-15 minute ka hota hai. Slow response = competitor ko chale jaate hain.

❌ Mistake 6: No Follow-up — Ek Message Ke Baad Chhodna
✅ Sahi: 3-message follow-up sequence — Day 1, Day 3, Day 7

💡 80% sales 5th follow-up ke baad hoti hain. First message se sirf 2% convert hote hain.

❌ Mistake 7: Status Pe Personal Content — Business Nahi
✅ Sahi: Status = free marketing — roz product/testimonial post karo

💡 Status pe selfies nahin — products dikhao. Contacts mein se 30-40% daily status dekhte hain.

❌ Mistake 8: Reviews Nahi Maangna
✅ Sahi: Har satisfied customer se Google review request bhejo

💡 Reviews = social proof = new customers without ads. 10 Google reviews = 3x zyada inquiries.
